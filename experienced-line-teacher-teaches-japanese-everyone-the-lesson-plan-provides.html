Methodology

I teach 2 courses related to Japanese : Japanese for Beginners and General Japanese. I will briefly describe them here.

Japanese for Beginners

I teach fundamental concepts of Japanese here, the ones students need to understand to be able to communicate in the language, thus acquiring working experience in the usage of various grammatical constructions, common vocabulary words, pronunciation, listening comprehension, speaking and writing skills. Students usually understand my explanations without difficulty since I speak several foreign languages and can compare their structures.

General Japanese

My approach to teaching General Japanese is to enable students continually advance in the language.
Grammar rules are thoroughly analyzed, reading, writing, speaking and listening activities are arranged with increasing difficulty levels.

I may combine these courses to do my best to deliver my lessons. However, it is the wish of a student to choose which course should be primarily taught.

Background

Hello. My name is Andrey. I graduated from a technical univestity and in a few years graduated from a linguistics university. So now I am a professional linguist with more than 18 year experience and I teach German, English, Russian and Japanese online.
