Concert pianist in Montreal teaches all levels and ages piano and music theory lessons

Methodology

I am a classically trained pianist, with many years of performing and would love to teach all levels and ages of students. I also would love to help with music theory as well! It is very essential to be able to communicate through music and I hope to foster this to anyone willing to learn.

Background

In 2019, I was one of the Canada National Gold Medalist and Graduates who achieved the highest academic credentials of the RCM Certificate Program. I am currently studying at the Schulich School of Music. I have attended multiple music festivals in Canada and America for solo and chamber music.
