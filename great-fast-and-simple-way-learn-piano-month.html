A great, fast and simple way to learn piano in a month

Methodology

I like to start off from the beginning dealing with knowing the notes, the white keys and the black keys. Once that is covered and the students are capable of telling the keys apart we will begin with chords. And then so forth.

Background

I am a musician, a singer, a pianist and more. I have been playing music for the last 15 years and I have taught for the last 5 years. The first instrument I learned was the piano, followed by the traditional flute, acoustic guitar, ukulele, saxophone, violin and of course vocals.
