MA Graduate Student in Psychology with focus in research methods, experimental design, data analysis, i.e., Psych Statistics.

Methodology

I am available for short-term and long-term tutoring projects. When tutoring students, I understand that my responsibilities may vary depending on the student's interests and requirements. I will collaborate with you to best identify skills you are interested in developing. This approach helps me cater to diverse learning styles and account for cultural and linguistic diversity, varied academic backgrounds, and career/academic goals.

I think being creative in providing multiple avenues to learn and to succeed engage students with different motivation needs. I also see formative feedback as a valuable resource for fellow students to acquire and develop a growth mindset. By identifying strengths and improvement areas related to students' work, students may become better equipped for future academic tasks.

Background

I am a 2nd year MA Psychology student. My interest and expertise are in research methodology, experimental design, and data analysis. In applied context, I research in the area of emotions, learning and motivational theories. I had plenty of experience tutoring others as my extracurricular activities, and I also have a lot of research experience as a psychology student. I am open to discuss anything related to psychology with you as long as you are interested!
