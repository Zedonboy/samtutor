UBC Master of Journalism graduate offers critique and guidance to students taking journalism courses

Methodology

Each student is different, so I'll be working with each one to cater our lessons to their needs. As with any form of communication, clarity and strategic structuring is key. I can help you to write effectively.

With me, you can expect:
- An assessment of your current skill level, goals, and timeline
- A flexible learning environment and plan
- Personalized tutoring style
- Help with research, wording, summation of ideas, etc.

No student will learn without the proper motivation and I believe having an instructor who cares about your learning is a key part in succeeding academically.
