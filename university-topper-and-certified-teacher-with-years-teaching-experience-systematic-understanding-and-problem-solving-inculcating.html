Prabhneet â€” I developed an avid interest in Mathematics and Physics during my high school days. In addition, my mother worked extremely hard in my primary school to make me both appreciate and excel in Maths. So the love for Maths, started extremely young and actually stayed with me all of my life.
QUESTION 02 | 05
2. What makes you passionate about your subject?
Prabhneet â€” Science just felt natural, logical and intuitive. Physics and Maths go hand in hand. While science may be the 'thought', maths is the 'language' to express it. My passion naturally took me on the road to a bachelor's in Physics followed with a Master's in Physics and since I continued with my love for Maths and Science, academic success easily followed. I wasn't simply studying for a career or a job- I was doing what I love.
QUESTION 03 | 05
3. What do you like about tutoring? What makes you a "Superprof"?
Prabhneet â€” Throughout my studying and subsequent teaching quests, I discovered that people often struggle with Science and even Maths because it's often presented in a very technical, difficult way. Mostly, students miss out on examples and are simply unable to imagine or visualise it. I wanted to teach Physics and Maths to bridge that disconnect. To make it accessible and digestible to everybody. I love inducing that look of wonder on a student's face when they truly completely understand something scientific and feel empowered and enlightened by that knowledge.
QUESTION 04 | 05
4. Do you have any role models; a teacher that inspired you?

QUESTION 05 | 05
5. What are your keys to success? *
Prabhneet â€” 1- Work at a problem till you solve it on your own. The longer you struggle at understanding something, the longer that knowlege is actually retained once you've understood it yourself.
