Piano and trumpet instructor, 10+ years teaching experience, Bachelor of Music from McGill University.

Methodology

Victoria is very engaging, warm, and loves to share her passion of music with students. She is very flexible and puts focus on getting to know each student individually, so that she may assist them in their personal needs and helping them in the best way possible. She is very open to what each student is interested in learning, while helping them find a balance with a strong technical foundation. Placing the most importance on what is best for the student, she uses her experience to adapt and tailor her teaching methods and style to fit their learning needs.

Background

Victoria has a Bachelor of Music in Jazz Trumpet Performance from McGill University. She studied for thirteen years with the Royal Conservatory of Music and has a grade 9 RCM in classical piano. Victoria has studied, performed, competed and taught in several provinces across the country including British Columbia, Alberta, Ontario and Quebec. She has experience playing and teaching a diverse array of genres including classical, jazz, pop and rock. She also has experience teaching students aged 5-50+ of all levels and abilities, in a private setting as well as in groups.
