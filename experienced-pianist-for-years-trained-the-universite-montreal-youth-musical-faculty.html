Methodology

I believe that every person has their own way of perceiving information and learning, which is why my teaching methodology is based on personalized teaching based on their learning style. Indeed, by observing the way a student absorbs information the easiest, I adapt my teaching methods to optimize the quality of his learning experience. My methodology also consists of balancing my focus on the students' strengths and weaknesses, so the student can progress efficiently without being discouraged.

Selon moi, chaque personne a une manière d'apprendre qui lui est propre. Ainsi, j'adapte ma manière d'enseigner aux besoins de l'élève et à sa façon d'apprendre pour optimiser la qualité de mon enseignement. Ma méthodologie consiste également à équilibrer mon focus sur les forces et les faiblesses de l'élève, afin que l'expérience ne lui soit ni décourageante, ni inefficace.
