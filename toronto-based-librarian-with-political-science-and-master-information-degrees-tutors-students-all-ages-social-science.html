Librarian with political science and information science degrees tutors students of all ages in social science subjects or research skills

Methodology

Meeting students at their current level, I use a constructionist pedagogical approach to address the subjects of immediate interest and relevance to my students through problem-based learning. By focusing on the concepts, topics and materials they need to master next, we can make progress together faster and help build momentum toward the kind of positive achievement that keeps students motivated to learn and excel.

Background

I am a professional librarian working in the legal information services sector. My academic background is in political science (undergraduate) and library and information science (graduate), and I am excited to work with students interested in improving their critical thinking or academic research skills to excel in any subject. I am also completing TESOL certification to work with ESL students.

Details

Please note that my ability to travel for in-home tutoring depends upon the actual public transit accessibility of students' specific locations. Therefore, some suburban locations may not be suitable for in-home visits. Also, if you are located outside of the radius indicated, I may still be able to reach you for sessions on weekday evenings as I work in downtown Toronto during the day.

Please notify me as soon as possible if you need to cancel so that we can re-schedule.

Greg's Resume

I am a researcher by training with experience teaching students and adult learners how to think about, access, understand, manipulate, and communicate information to achieve their goals. This includes research strategies, proper citation (and avoiding plagiarism!), effective editing, information literacy, and much more.

Instruction can be tailored specifically to address identified performance issues and to reinforce the outcomes of students' current school/academic coursework, or designed to provide additional enrichment based on alternative subjects of particular interest to avid learners.
