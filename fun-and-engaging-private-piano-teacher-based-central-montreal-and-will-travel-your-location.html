Fun and Engaging Private Piano Teacher. Based in Central Montreal and will travel to your location.

Methodology

I am classically trained but can also teach Jazz methods. I adapt my lessons with each individual student to work towards achieving their own personal goals, whether that's advancing through a grade structure or just playing for fun. I am very personable and have an approachable teaching style. Punctualilty, oragnisation and reliability are key aspects of my work ethic.

Background

I am a passionate piano tutor who has been teaching professionally for eight years. I have just relocated from London, United Kingdom where I had a teaching portfolio of almost 40 students of varying ages and abilities. I hold a Bachelor of Music degree from Trinity Music College, London.
