Anthropologist, ethnologist (East Asia), with previous training in classical letters and philosophy;

Methodology

My method is based on example, and personal commitment to provide students with the tools they lack to progress in their studies, and / or complete their composition and writing work (dissertations, reports, theses). I start by probing the skills of the students, before providing them with the methodological frameworks, as well as the possible documentation, which they may lack. I suggest, correct, reread, rewrite even if necessary; I work in the exchange and whole sharing of knowledge.

Ambassador

One of our best! High-quality profile, qualifications verified and responds during the day. Brigitte will plan your first lesson with care.

Background

I am a professor emeritus of the universities (more than 30 years of experience between the universities of Montpellier, Paris and Lille). Professional ethnologist, internationally recognized in his field, my classic course (grandes écoles) is punctuated by international activities (student-teacher exchanges via Erasmus Mundus programs, in particular; participation in conferences and seminars, research directions). It allows me to intervene in the fields of comparative literature, philosophy and epistemology, social anthropology (with a specialty in the Himalayan regions - Nepal, Tibet / China, Sikkim / India).
