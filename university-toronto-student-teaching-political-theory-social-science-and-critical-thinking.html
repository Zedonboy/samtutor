University of Toronto Student Teaching Political Theory, Social Science and Critical Thinking

Methodology

Political theory and science should not revolve around imposing political views, partisan ideas or ideological views. Political science students are renowned for their critical thinking abilities, problem solving and ability to understand complicated ideas and issues. A good political theory class is revolves around the process of theory building and case study, not the answer or judgements that arise.

Background

As an IB graduate and current University of Toronto student majoring in Political Science, Sociology, Semiotics and Communications, I am well versed in political and sociological theory while having experience in a multitude of teaching methods both as a tutor and student.
