UBC engineering student tutoring math up to high school level in Calgary

Methodology

My teaching method is to push students to adopt a healthy and efficient study habit. As an individual that has been tutored many times, I recognized that if tutoring is not done right, it can cause students to become lazy and forget how to study by themselves. This is very troublesome for post-secondary students because, by then, it is difficult to find tutors, and most likely more expensive.

Background

I am currently a student at UBC for engineering, but due to the pandemic, I have been unable to study at the campus and will have to complete online classes for this year. I wanted to make online classes as an opportunity to tutor children and give them access to the education that they may be missing due to the situation. Please contact me for more info, like the UBC acceptance letter and official high school transcript. Thank you:)
